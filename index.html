How to make the worlds best peanut butter and jelly sandwhich

Tired of dry, bready peanutbutter and jelly sandwhiches? you came to the right place me friend

step 1: put 2 pieces of any kind of bread in the toaster

step 2:set the toaster on medium heat for 2 minutes

step 3:take toast out and put 2 scoops jelly on both sides

step 4: get a piece of none toasted bread and put peanut butter on both sides
step 5:put he doubled sided peanut butter bread on top of one of the jelly toasts then do the same with the other jelly toast
maybe you dont like jelly? its all good honey is a great substitute. here is a way to make a great peanut butter and honey sandwhich.

step 1= spread honey on two pieces of toast step 2= spread peanut butter on another piece of bread, (make sure you dont put the honey and peanut butter together because they dont mix that well). step 3= put the honey toast, honey side down on the peanut butter sandwhich. step 4= cut the bread diagonal and your set
